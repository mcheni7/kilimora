Tour Overview

Experience the best of Tanzania's wildlife in this compact 2-day safari. Witness the massive herds of elephants in Tarangire National Park, known as the "Elephant Playground," and explore the diverse landscapes of Lake Manyara, home to the famous tree-climbing lions and flocks of pink flamingos. This tour is perfect for those with limited time who still want a genuine safari experience.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arusha to Tarangire National Park

Our guide will pick you up from your lodge early in the morning followed by a 2-hour drive to Tarangire National Park after briefing. The park is famous for its majestic baobab trees, often known as "trees of life," which dot the landscape and add to the beauty of Tarangire's surroundings. The Tarangire River, the park's primary source of water, draws hundreds of species, especially during the dry season.

When you arrive in Tarangire, you will go on a game drive in search of the park's wildlife, which includes lesser kudus, buffalos, oryx, elands, giraffes, and zebras. You might encounter predators like lions, leopards, and cheetahs if you're lucky. After a brief game drive, you will have lunch in one of the park's picnic spots. You will then go on an afternoon game drive to look for more wildlife and other park attractions. In the evening, you will drive to the lodge in Karatu for dinner and an overnight stay.

  • Main Destination: Tarangire National Park
  • Meals & Drinks: All Meals Included

Day 2: Lake Manyara National Park - Transfer to Arusha

Following breakfast, you will drive to Lake Manyara for morning game drive, often referred to as the realm of tree-climbing lions, a compact yet incredibly varied park. Its prominent feature is a vast salt lake, ebbing and flowing with the seasons, providing residence to numerous flamingos and more than 300 other bird varieties.

The park's diverse terrain encompasses open grasslands, woodland teeming with primates, and cliffs adorned with iconic baobab trees. After the morning wildlife drive, you will be driven back to Arusha/Kilimanjaro to catch your flight back home/next destination. Thank you for choosing Ecentric Adventure Tours, hope you enjoyed staying with us.

  • Main Destination: Lake Manyara National Park
  • Meals & Drinks: All Meals Included
