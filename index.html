About Kilimora Adventure

Where Passion Meets the Wild

Kilimora Adventure is an emerging premier tour operator based in the heart of Arusha, Tanzania. Founded in 2025, our story is rooted in a deep love for the African wilderness and a commitment to authentic exploration.

The company was born from the combined vision of two dedicated founders: Franklin E. Moshi, a veteran tour guide and driver with more than 5 years of "boots-on-the-ground" experience, and Reservations Thomas Mungure, who left a career in non-funded projects under USAID & CDC where she served for more than 10 years, to follow her true calling in tourism. Together, they turned their shared passion into a mission: to provide guests with more than just a tour, but a life-changing journey.

Destinations to Inspire

Serengeti

Discover the legendary Serengeti, a land where nature unfolds in its most dramatic form. From the world-famous Great Migration to endless golden plains filled with wildlife, Serengeti National Park offers a truly timeless safari experience unlike any other.

→ EXPLORE SERENGETI

Ngorongoro

Explore the wonders of the Ngorongoro Conservation Area, home to the breathtaking Ngorongoro Crater — a natural wildlife sanctuary unlike anywhere else on earth. Discover dramatic landscapes, abundant wildlife, and a harmony of nature that defines this World Heritage Site.

→ EXPLORE NGORONGORO

Mount Kilimanjaro

Conquer the majestic Mount Kilimanjaro — Africa’s highest peak and one of the world’s most iconic trekking destinations. From lush rainforests to breathtaking glaciers, every step reveals a new world of natural beauty and adventure.

→ CLIMB KILIMANJARO

Tarangire

Experience the charm of Tarangire National Park — famous for its towering baobab trees, vast elephant herds, and untouched wilderness. Discover a peaceful yet wildlife-rich haven perfect for authentic safari adventures.
