11-Day The Great Tanzania & Zanzibar

Immerse yourself in the ultimate African experience with this 11-day tour. Start with a thrilling 6-day wildlife safari through Northern Tanzania’s most famous parks—Tarangire, Lake Manyara, Serengeti, and Ngorongoro. Then, fly to the Spice Island of Zanzibar for 5 days of relaxation, culture, and beach bliss.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arusha to Tarangire National Park

Depart Arusha for Tarangire National Park. Famous for its high density of elephants and baobab trees, Tarangire is a spectacular start to your safari. Enjoy a game drive and witness the diverse wildlife gathering at the river. Dinner and overnight at a lodge/camp.

  • Main Destination: Tarangire National Park
  • Meals & Drinks: Lunch & Dinner Included

Day 2: Tarangire to Lake Manyara

Short drive to Lake Manyara National Park. Explore the groundwater forests, home to blue monkeys and bushbucks. Look out for the famous tree-climbing lions and the vast flocks of flamingos on the lake shore. Dinner and overnight in Karatu.

  • Main Destination: Lake Manyara National Park
  • Meals & Drinks: All Meals Included

Day 3: Karatu to Serengeti National Park

Travel to the world-renowned Serengeti National Park. Pass through the Ngorongoro Conservation Area, with opportunities to see Maasai villages. Enter the Serengeti plains and enjoy a game drive en route to your camp. Dinner and overnight in Serengeti.

  • Main Destination: Serengeti National Park
  • Meals & Drinks: All Meals Included

Day 4: Full Day in Serengeti

Dedicate the entire day to exploring the Serengeti. Your guide will take you to the best spots to see the Big Five and the Great Migration (seasonal). Enjoy a picnic lunch in the bush surrounded by nature. Dinner and overnight at your camp.

  • Main Destination: Serengeti National Park
  • Meals & Drinks: All Meals Included

Day 5: Serengeti to Ngorongoro Crater Rim

Morning game drive in Serengeti before departing for the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. Arrive at your lodge on the crater rim in time for sunset. The view of the crater bowl is breathtaking. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.

  • Main Destination: Ngorongoro Conservation Area
  • Meals & Drinks: All Meals Included

Day 6: Ngorongoro Crater to Zanzibar

Descend into the crater for a half-day tour. Spot lions, rhinos, and flamingos in this unique ecosystem. After a picnic lunch, drive to Arusha or Kilimanjaro Airport for your evening flight to Zanzibar. Upon arrival in Zanzibar, transfer to your hotel in Stone Town.

  • Main Destination: Stone Town, Zanzibar
  • Meals & Drinks: Breakfast & Lunch Included

Day 7: Stone Town Tour & Spice Farm

Explore the winding alleys of Stone Town, visiting the House of Wonders, the Old Fort, and the slave market. In the afternoon, take a spice tour to smell and taste the island's famous cloves, nutmeg, and cinnamon. Transfer to your beach resort in Nungwi or Kendwa.

  • Main Destination: Zanzibar Beach Resort
  • Meals & Drinks: Breakfast & Dinner Included

Day 8: Zanzibar Beach Relaxation (or Jozani Forest)

Relax on the pristine beaches. Alternatively, take a half-day trip to Jozani Chwaka Bay National Park to see the rare Red Colobus monkeys diverse mangrove forests. Return to your resort for dinner.

  • Main Destination: Zanzibar
  • Meals & Drinks: Breakfast & Dinner Included

Day 9: Leisure at Beach Resort

Enjoy a full day at leisure. Swim in the turquoise waters, sunbathe, or enjoy water sports such as snorkeling, diving, or a sunset dhow cruise (optional). Indulge in fresh seafood and island vibes.

  • Main Destination: Zanzibar
  • Meals & Drinks: Breakfast & Dinner Included

Day 10: Leisure at Beach Resort

Your final full day in paradise. Relax by the pool, walk along the beach, or visit the local village aquarium to swim with turtles (optional). Enjoy a farewell dinner at the resort.

  • Main Destination: Zanzibar
  • Meals & Drinks: Breakfast & Dinner Included

Day 11: Departure

After breakfast, check out of your hotel. Depending on your flight schedule, you will be transferred to Zanzibar International Airport for your onward journey, marking the end of your Great Tanzania & Zanzibar adventure.

  • Main Destination: Zanzibar Airport
  • Meals & Drinks: Breakfast Included
