About Us

About Kilimora Adventure

Where Passion Meets the Wild

Kilimora Adventure is an emerging premier tour operator based in the heart of Arusha, Tanzania. Founded in 2025, our story is rooted in a deep love for the African wilderness and a commitment to authentic exploration.

The company was born from the combined vision of two dedicated founders: Franklin E. Moshi, a veteran tour guide and driver with more than 5 years of "boots-on-the-ground" experience, and Sarah Thomas, who left a career in non-funded projects under USAID & CDC where she served for more than 10 years, to follow her true calling in tourism. Together, they turned their shared passion into a mission: to provide guests with more than just a tour, but a life-changing journey.

Why Journey With Us?

Since our inception, we have grown into one of Africa's premier safari operators by staying true to our core values:

  • Expert Knowledge: Our guides are more than just drivers; they are storytellers and experts who bring Tanzania's breathtaking landscapes and wildlife to life.
  • Personalized Touch: From classic safaris to custom-designed luxury itineraries, we ensure every trip feels personal and meaningful.
  • Authentic Connections: We don't just show you the sights; we help you create genuine connections with the rich culture and natural beauty of Tanzania.
Our Vision

To become Tanzania's most trusted and innovative tour guide company, delivering authentic, sustainable, and unforgettable travel experiences that showcase the nation's natural beauty, rich wildlife, and vibrant cultural heritage to the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create memorable and responsible travel experiences in Tanzania through expert guiding, personalized service, and a deep respect for nature, culture, and local communities.

Our Objectives

Driven by Excellence & Responsibility

  • Delivering safe, reliable, and expertly guided tours across Tanzania's most iconic destinations, including national parks, cultural heritage sites, and adventure locations.
  • Building a strong and trusted brand recognized for professionalism, innovation, and exceptional customer experiences within the tourism industry.
  • Promoting sustainable and responsible tourism practices that preserve wildlife, natural ecosystems, and cultural heritage.
  • Creating long-lasting partnerships with clients, travel agencies, and hospitality providers both locally and internationally.
  • Continuously enhancing service quality through staff training, innovation, and customer feedback.
  • Making a positive impact on local communities by supporting responsible tourism and community-based initiatives.
Service Standards

Our Commitment to Excellence

At Kilimora Adventure, our service standards define our commitment to excellence, safety, and responsible tourism. We strive to deliver exceptional travel experiences by upholding the following principles:

  • Professional & Licensed Guides: Our tours are led by highly trained, knowledgeable, and fully licensed guides who are customer-focused and passionate about delivering enriching experiences.
  • Safety as a Top Priority: We adhere strictly to established safety procedures and internationally recognized tourism standards to ensure the well-being of our clients at all times.
  • Personalized Travel Experiences: Every journey is thoughtfully customized to reflect our clients' interests, budgets, and preferred travel styles, ensuring a unique and memorable experience.
  • Reliability & Punctuality: We provide consistent and dependable service, maintaining professionalism and timeliness from the booking process through the completion of each tour.
  • Clear & Transparent Communication: We ensure honest pricing, accurate travel information, and responsive customer support to build trust and confidence with our clients.
  • Sustainable & Ethical Tourism Practices: Our operations prioritize environmental responsibility, wildlife conservation, and ethical engagement with local communities.
